About

I'm a builder-operator. I've spent two decades leading strategy, product, and growth at AT&T, Amazon, RealNetworks, and startups across 20+ countries. That experience shapes how I think about AI: not just what to build, but how entire ecosystems develop — the products, the standards, the infrastructure, the incentive systems — broad human flourishing in many places, not just the markets that can already afford it.

Today I advise BCG on AI product strategy for social impact, and I explore these ideas in AI for Human Flourishing.
